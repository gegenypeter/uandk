OPTIMISED FOR INSTALLATION

uandksound outdoor loudspeakers have been designed to blend in to outdoor environments. Available in white/black or green finishes the look of these loudspeakers will compliment landscaping designs and disappear into their surroundings. Each loudspeaker cabinet houses two drivers set at precise angles so you experience consistent stereo effects as you move around outdoors. Once mounted, the loudspeaker can be easily pivoted to direct the sound where you want it, and they will stay where you position them and not droop or slip over time.

Our loudspeakers utilize a durable weather-resistant construction that is built to withstand the harshest outdoor environments for a long time. Most discerning customers will appreciate the engineered construction for high-humidity ready to survive extreme temperatures while maintaining superior sound quality. uandksound loudspeakers feature internal wire connections to eliminate corrosion caused by moisture and to prevent damage from garden tools. Moreover uandksound offers an associated balanced power amplifier VTA-240F II to get the best sound performance of each loudspeaker under the hardest environmental conditions.

You can enjoy clear, natural sound from an outside loudspeaker enclosure that easily mounts to walls in your deck, garden or pool area.
