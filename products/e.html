THE SOUND OF INVISIBILITY

Our new E Series features allow uandksound a new standard in acoustical performance to in-wall and in-ceiling applications. Designed for those seeking the world’s most articulate and dynamic multi-channel home theater systems, or simply a stealthy 2-channel system, the E Series speakers are voice and timbre matched with one another, as well as the rest of the uandksound products.

All models feature an ultra-low profile, micro perforated grille, which are also paintable for a near invisible integration into any wall or ceiling. Grills firmly attached with neodymium magnets. E Series also include extra wide threaded screws for quick dog leg fastening. A magnetic system secures grilles easily into place, reducing the risk of warping or damage during installation.

Some of the key features are the following; Smooth Visual Flangeless System, PRX™ Poly Resin CrossPoint Molds, PRX™ dog legs, Never Rust™ grills firmly attached with neodymium magnets besides extra wide threaded screws for quick installation and push gold plated binding posts.
