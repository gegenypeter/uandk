Beautiful design is the meaning of perfection

There is no greater comfort than sitting on the sofa to watch a movie or listening music of the most relaxed way. We are constantly working to provide the best listening experience and create the same feeling of a live musical or film performance. Uandksound knows the importance of feeling good at home surrounded by a pleasant environment where everything maintains an aesthetic coherence. We have always keen on to design products with a double function, functional and aesthetic.

Fashions change and adapting to them is very important, for this reason we have worked on the new M5 series, an evolution of the successful M3, but adapted to more discerning domestic environments where not only aesthetics prevails also an avantgarde sound prepared to reproduce high-resolution audio from the best films and music recordings. M5 Series are engineered with great care to integrate their visual presence in your interior.

The result is a refined entertainment experience without disrupting the beauty of your surroundings. All our products and components are proudly designed in Europe and our skilled and well trained engineers test every component carefully.

Audiophile Performance

The new internals for the M5 series include an improved crossover that provides excellent linear response through a high-density structured cabinet to get an outstanding dynamic range, especially for home theater applications. Our renowned Air Motion ribbon tweeter is already implemented in many of our loudspeakers like M5 series. A more defined response and extended high frequency is only achieved with the most advanced components.

The internal structure of M5 Series has been developed in such a way that parallel surfaces are avoided inside to achieve a better sound diffraction and offering less distortion with high sound pressure levels.

M5 Series family consists of front, center and surround channels, also a dedicated active subwoofer. All this makes this equipment the perfect fashion loudspeakers solution to enjoy the best sound at home without the need of complex installations. Our timeless design will be the best warranty to enjoy the best sound in harmony without disrupting your home decoration for a long time.
