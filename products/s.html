THE PINNACLE OF HIGH-PERFORMANCE AUDIO

Key to their sophisticated sound is an advanced crossover network. Each loudspeaker crossover is built with the highest-quality components. Internals including massive air-core coils, thick, high-purity copper wire, and low-distortion capacitors ensure that every nuance of the audio signal is preserved.

Designed to excel in a wide variety of sound reinforcement applications, the full range S Series are compact wall mount loudspeaker that provides inspiring acoustic performance with exceptional clarity and articulation. Manufactured to the highest standards of quality, these products are available through authorized specialty custom installation specialists and carry the full uandksound warranty.

Critical to architectural products is their ease of installation. The S Series continues uandksound tradition of innovating engineering solutions which allow custom retailers to quickly and easily install their products. Each model features premium MDF enclosure and an extremely robust mounting-lock system that couples with the speaker’s rigid chassis when installed. S series models allow installers to complete horizontal or vertical installation.

The standard finish for this product is black matte.
